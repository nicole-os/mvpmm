VELA ANALYTICS — MESSAGING FRAMEWORK
=====================================

COMPANY OVERVIEW
----------------
Vela Analytics is a supply chain intelligence platform that helps mid-market
manufacturers and distributors predict disruptions before they happen. By
combining real-time supplier data with AI-driven risk modeling, Vela gives
operations and procurement teams a single source of truth for the decisions
that keep product moving.

Founded in 2021. Headquartered in Austin, TX. Serving customers across
industrial manufacturing, consumer goods, and specialty retail.

KEY MESSAGES
------------
1. EARLY WARNING, NOT JUST REPORTING
   Vela surfaces supplier risk signals 2–4 weeks before they show up in
   your ERP. That's enough time to qualify alternates, adjust production
   schedules, or have a conversation with the supplier — not just file
   an incident report.

2. BUILT FOR THE TEAM IN THE MIDDLE
   Procurement, operations, and logistics teams don't have data science
   resources. Vela is designed for the people who need answers on Monday
   morning, not a new analytics platform to maintain.

3. WORKS WITH WHAT YOU ALREADY HAVE
   Vela integrates with SAP, Oracle, NetSuite, and major EDI providers
   in under two weeks. No rip-and-replace. No six-month implementation.

4. TRUSTED DATA, EXPLAINABLE SIGNALS
   Every risk flag in Vela links back to the underlying source — whether
   that's a supplier financial filing, a port congestion index, or a
   weather event. Your team sees the signal and the reason.
